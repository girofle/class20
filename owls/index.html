Nocturnal Birds Owls are divided into two families: the Strigidae family of true (or typical) owls; and the Tytonidae family of barn-owls.

Something About Owls

So. Owl's are cool. We used to have people from the local nature reserve come to our Elementary school every 6 months or so. They always brought an Owl.

Barn Owls

Did you know that there are only 2 distinct branches of owl taxonomy? Barn owls are distinctly different from all other kinds of owls.

Look at this owl!

There are even Owls that rarely fly. They burrow into the ground like carnivorous meercats with feathers. This is not that owl.

Something else about owls

I once saw a video where someone made a camera stabilization system by attatching a GoPro to an owl's head, and the owl naturally stabilized the movements. Looking back it may have been a chicken.
